    Instructions

    (30 min - 1 hour)
    • make sure you have all the materials (don't gather yet)
    • clear out driveway
    • set up ramps and drive onto them
    • set a timer for 10 minutes for engine to cool off
    • while waiting, gather the materials
    • put cardboard down and pan into place
    • Highlander: oil pan is behind front passenger wheel
    • once timer is done, take ratchet and unscrew drain
    • wait for oil to drain
    • while waiting, wipe off screw and put on new gasket
    • once done draining, screw drain bolt back in
    • unscrew the oil filter
    • filter is sideways on the front of the engine block on Highlander
    • wipe off dripped oil, lubricate edge of new filter and screw on
    • double check oil drain screw and filter connections
    • pour in new oil
    • Highlander: 4.1 quarts
    • Fusion: 4.5 quarts
    • check new oil level
    • run car and make sure nothing blows up
    • put materials away
    • order new materials for next time
    • update this page
