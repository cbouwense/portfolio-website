How to cook steak on a charcoal grill

December 2, 2025
  1. Take the grill out and remove any previous ashes.
  2. Scrape and wipe down the grates.
  3. Put the bottom grate back in and get the briquettes started in the chimney filled up.
    • This will probably take 15-30 minutes to fully start all of them.
